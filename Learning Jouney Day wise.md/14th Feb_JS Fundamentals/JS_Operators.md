# JavaScript Operators

## **Arithmetic Operators**
- `+` Addition
- `-` Subtraction
- `*` Multiplication
- `/` Division
- `%` Modulus (remainder)
- `**` Exponentiation

## **Comparison Operators**
- `==` Equal to (loose equality)
- `===` Equal to (strict equality)
- `!=` Not equal to (loose)
- `!==` Not equal to (strict)
- `<` Less than
- `>` Greater than
- `<=` Less than or equal to
- `>=` Greater than or equal to

## **Logical Operators**
- `&&` AND
- `||` OR
- `!` NOT

## **Assignment Operators**
- `=` Assign
- `+=` Add and assign
- `-=` Subtract and assign
- `*=` Multiply and assign
- `/=` Divide and assign
- `%=` Modulus and assign
- `**=` Exponentiation and assign

## **Bitwise Operators**
- `&` AND
- `|` OR
- `^` XOR
- `~` NOT
- `<<` Left shift
- `>>` Right shift
- `>>>` Unsigned right shift

## **Other Useful Operators**
- `?:` Ternary conditional
- `??` Nullish coalescing
- `?.` Optional chaining
- `...` Spread operator
- `typeof` Type checking
- `instanceof` Instance checking
